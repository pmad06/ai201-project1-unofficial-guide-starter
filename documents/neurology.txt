Popular Restaurants
A Culinary Hub of Local Favorites
Gainesville, FL is a food lover’s paradise, offering a diverse array of popular local restaurants that reflect the city’s vibrant culture and farm-to-table ethos. From cozy cafes and classic Southern diners to modern bistros and international eateries, the city serves up flavors for every palate. Standout spots like Satchel’s Pizza, The Top, and Emmet’s Social Club showcase creative menus made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, while countless hidden gems offer everything from gourmet sandwiches to artisanal desserts. Beyond downtown, nearby neighborhoods and surrounding towns feature charming eateries, food trucks, and specialty markets that make exploring Gainesville’s culinary scene an adventure in itself. Whether you’re craving hearty comfort food, innovative cuisine, or a relaxing brunch, Gainesville delivers a memorable dining experience at every turn.

One Love Cafe
One Love Café offers a spacious outdoor patio seating area, a massive pet-friendly field for running & playing, fire pits, a community garden, a stage for live music, TVs for showing the UF Game, a kid friendly outdoor environment, a full-service bar and more.

Stachel’s Pizza
Satchel’s Pizza opened on March 7th, 2003. The mission is to make great food in a beautiful atmosphere and serve up something for all the senses.
Eat in a van, under a plane, or in a greenhouse. Enjoy Satch made stained glass windows, local art, homemade sodas, and live music. There’s a playground for the kids and a friendly smile all around. 

Dragonfly Sushi & Sake
Our motivation – Kaizen—to always be improving. The spirit that drives us. Fish flown in from Japan. Produce from the farm. Everything fresh. Our reputation rests on the creativity of every dish and drink. We find joy in every meaningful connection. And purpose in every detail.

Prime and Pearl
Prime & Pearl is dedicated to preparing the finest dishes and offering impeccable service. By design, our seating is somewhat limited so we can ensure every guest’s dining experience is unique and special. Our focus is on bringing things together -often unexpected things – to make something creative and authentic. We believe a sense of wonder is an integral part of every memorable dining experience in our space.


Spurrier’s Gridiron Grille
“The other day someone asked me what my restaurant is like. I told them to roll all my interviews into one. Then take the most renowned regional football museum for miles and add all-American food that rivals the best in the city. It’s something like that.”
– Steve Spurrier.

Boca Fiesta
We use the guiding principle of trying to create the kind of place we’d want to go to, the kind of place everybody will feel welcome, because we wouldn’t want even a single soul to face extinction due to insufficient Taco Consumption. We all have to eat food every day for the rest of our lives, so we might as well have some fun in the process!

Embers Prime Steaks Wood Grill
High-end chophouse serves surf ‘n’ turf & a broad wine list with a full bar & outdoor seating.

Paramount Grille
Fine Dining. Relaxed Atmosphere. Classic space with understated decor serves Mediterranean-inspired cuisine.

Marks Prime Steakhouse
Mark’s Prime Steakhouse has a goal to create a unique dining experience that will please the palate and soothe the soul. We serve the finest beef, the freshest seafood, premium wines and naturally fresh vegetables.

The Top
Hip, casual spot serving burgers, beer & modern American fare, with vegan & vegetarian options.

Amelia’s Italian Restaurant
Casual, enduring bistro with old-world flair offering homemade pasta & other classic Italian dishes.

Blue Gill Quality Food
Our mission is to serve fresh, delicious food made from the best local ingredients. Our gorgeous copper top bar and spacious dining area make Blue Gill a great place for friends, family, and large parties to enjoy food inspired by southern style cooking. So come sample our great tasting combinations of food and spirits in a casual, fun atmosphere.

Mojo Hogtown Bar-B-Que
MOJO BBQ is a premier barbecue, southern food, and craft cocktail destination. Barbecue, blues, and bourbon — three distinctly American products — come together at MOJO to offer a good-natured atmosphere.

Chopstix Cafe
Lively eatery offering an eclectic menu that includes dishes from China, Korea, Japan & Thailand.


Yamoto
Casual steakhouse offering table-side grilling, a sushi bar & other Japanese fare. We strive to provide the freshest ingredients made to order. Come and watch your meal go up in flame right at your table or experience our sushi chef’s art in the form of classic renditions!

Mi Apa Latin Cafe
At Mi Apá Latin Café, our goal is to serve up traditional Cuban food using nothing but the freshest, tastiest ingredients. We’re bringing back authentic, old world Cuban recipes and making them affordable for our customers.


Bangkok Square Authentic Thai Cuisine
Our goal at Bangkok Square Restaurant is to be a place where you always know you will get the best authentic fine Thai cuisine. Enjoy a relaxing and pleasant atmosphere while indulging in our delicious selections. We enjoy sharing the most fragrant, interesting, and flavorful dishes from all over Thailand.


Public and General
Public & General is a self-service restaurant and tavern located in the heart of the Northeast of Gainesville. The eatery’s lunch menu offers specialty sandwiches, a cheese & charcuterie selection, along with various other daily plates. After 5 o’clock, our dinner menu expands to include our house prepared small plates. Our bar offers a large selection of international wines, fine spirits, cocktails and craft beers.


Cintrón – A Floribbean Restaurant
Floribbean cuisine is comprised of flavors that are influenced by Caribbean, Asian, and Hispanic populations that emigrate to Florida. When eating a Floribbean meal, expect the use of exotic spices, perfectly seasoned proteins, and a beautifully presented dish.
