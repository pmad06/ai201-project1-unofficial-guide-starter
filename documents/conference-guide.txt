Guide for the 2020 Pedagogy, Practice and Philosophy Conference 
 February 1, 2020Pugh Hall on the University of Florida Campus 
  Welcome to the University of Florida! We hope you enjoy your stay in Gainesville. 
  The following will help you navigate the campus and surrounding area. 
  If you have any questions feel free to ask.  
  Getting Here: Gainesville has a local airport for convenient flights or visitors can fly into Orlando or Jacksonville, Florida and then rent a car and drive into town. 
  From Orlando, the drive is approximately two hours, while Jacksonville is approximately one hour and 45 minutes away.  
  Map of the University of Florida: Use one of the following maps to get around campus https://campusmap.ufl.edu/#/index http://www.ufl.edu/about/maps-directions/  
  Gainesville also has a public bus system that you can ride to get around town. Although most locations are walkable from campus. 
  If you are driving in, you are welcome to park in several of the lots near Pugh Hall or across the street from the University. 
  Places to Stay:  Gainesville is very walkable and has public transit (RTS bus) that can take you from the UF campus to downtown. 
  Uber and Lyft are also available. The following hotel recommendations are all within 1 mile of UF and have free wifi.  
  • Hampton Inn and Suites – Downtown – good location downtown, close to restaurants and bars, 14 blocks from Pugh Hall  
  • Holiday Inn – University Center, right across from the University, walking distance to Pugh  
  • Reitz Union Hotel – on campus – walking distance to Pugh Hall  
  • Hilton – University of Florida Conference Center – west side of campus – can take a bus to Pugh Hall  
  Places to Eat:  Gainesville has great local restaurants downtown or you can grab something closer to campus 
  For places to eat on campus see http://gatordining.com
